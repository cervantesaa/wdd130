Physical therapy

Physical rehabilitation

It is beneficial for relaxing the muscles in order to relieve the tensions that stress, lack of rest or bad postures can cause in them. This massage is not only highly recommended for those people who, due to excessive physical exercise, may have injured a muscle, but also for those other people who, due to a sedentary lifestyle, force certain positions or adopt other inconvenient ones.

Massage post-surgery

It stimulates the function of the lymphatic system helping to eliminate toxins, decreasing inflammation and fluid retention, activating circulation and strengthening the immune system. Also indicated for weight loss and anti-cellulite treatments as well as for people who are sitting or standing for a long time.
